## 🌐 WEBSITE & APP BRAND RULES

### Color Palette (from logo)
| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Blue | Deep navy | `#1a3a6e` | Headers, buttons, links |
| Primary Red | Bright red | `#e63946` | CTAs, highlights, accents |
| Secondary Grey | Cool grey | `#6c757d` | Body text, captions |
| Background | White | `#ffffff` | Page background |
| Dark | Near black | `#1a1a1a` | Footer, dark sections |

- **Headings:** Noto Sans Bengali (Bangla) + Inter (English)

- Website: Top-left, 150px wide, linked to homepage
- Mobile: 120px wide, hamburger menu right
- Loading screen: Centered logo + "Keystone Education" text
- Favicon: Use the airplane icon only (cropped from logo)

### Founder Photo Placement
- About page: Large photo (400px wide) + bio text to the right
- Mobile: Photo full width above bio
- Caption: "Hasibul Munim, Founder & CEO, 9 Years in South Korea"
