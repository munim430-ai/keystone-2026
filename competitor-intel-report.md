## TIER 3 — THE FRAUD CARTEL (Your Real Enemy)

**The Financial Express investigation (May 2026) confirms:**
- No mandatory registration for education consultancies in Bangladesh
- BAIRA exists but only for labour migration (2,400 members)

1. Quote ৳20,000–50,000 base fee (looks cheap)

3. Final bill: ৳250,000–400,000

**Why this is your weapon:** Every student who was burned by these guys is a potential Keystone customer who will pay MORE for transparency, not less. The market doesn't need cheaper. It needs TRUSTED.
