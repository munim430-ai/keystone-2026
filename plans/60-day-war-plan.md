## THE ROOT CAUSE

You placed 1 student in 4 months because you were lazy. Not because the market is bad. Not because competitors are strong. Not because you lack skills. **You had time. You chose to build software instead of making calls.**

Software is comfortable. Calls are uncomfortable. Building feels like progress. Selling feels like rejection. **But only selling pays rent.**

This plan treats laziness as the enemy. Every system is designed to make the default action = selling.

## THE LAZINESS ANTIDOTE

### The 2-Minute Rule
Every sales task must start within 2 minutes of deciding to do it. No "let me check something first." No "after I finish this." Pick up the phone and dial within 2 minutes.

### The Streak System
Track your streak on a physical calendar. Every day you hit 10 calls = green dot. Every day you miss = red dot. Your goal is 30 green dots in a row. One red dot resets the streak. Print the calendar. Stick it on your wall. Take a photo every Sunday and send it to your accountability partner.

### The Pain-Pleasure Balance
**Pleasure:** Every 10-call day, you get 30 minutes of "build time" (coding, product, whatever you want). This is your reward. No calls = no build time.
**Pain:** If you miss 2 days in a week, you must call 20 people the next day. No sleep until it's done.

### The Public Commitment
Post on your personal Facebook: "I am committing to 10 sales calls per day for the next 60 days to grow Keystone Education. I was lazy before. Not anymore. Hold me accountable." Tag 3 friends. The social pressure will force you to execute.

### The Marina Mirror
Marina is watching you. If she sees the founder making excuses, she will make excuses. If she sees the founder grinding, she will grind. **You are the pace car.**

## THE DAILY BATTLE PLAN (Founder)

### Morning Routine (08:00–10:00)
- 08:00 — Wake up. No phone scrolling. No news. No YouTube.
- 08:15 — Shower. Dress like you are going to an office. (Even if you work from home. Psychology matters.)
- 08:30 — Breakfast. No phone.
- 08:45 — Open lead tracker. Review yesterday's calls. Plan today's 10.
- 09:00 — **FIRST CALL.** No warm-up. No "let me check something." Dial immediately.

### The 10-Call Block (10:00–14:00)
- 10:00–11:00 — Calls 1–3
- 11:00–11:15 — Break. Check WhatsApp. Respond to urgent messages ONLY.
- 11:15–12:15 — Calls 4–6
- 12:15–13:00 — Lunch. No phone.
- 13:00–14:00 — Calls 7–10

**Rules during call block:**
- Phone on Do Not Disturb except Marina and family.
- No Facebook. No coding. No "research."
- If a call goes to voicemail, leave a message and move to the next.
- If someone says "call me later," schedule it and move to the next.
- The goal is 10 DIALS, not 10 answers. Dials = effort. Answers = luck.

### Afternoon (15:00–18:00)
- 15:00–16:00 — Follow up on calls from morning. WhatsApp messages. Facebook replies.
- 16:00–17:00 — College visit OR IELTS institute visit OR content creation.
- 17:00–18:00 — Marina check-in. Review her calls. Coach. Adjust.

### Evening (19:00–21:00)
- 19:00–20:00 — Dinner. Family time. No work.
- 20:00–20:30 — Post on Facebook. Schedule tomorrow's post.
- 20:30–21:00 — **DAILY REPORT.** Write in a notebook or Google Sheet:
  - Calls made: ___
  - Leads generated: ___
  - Posts published: ___
  - Revenue collected: ___
  - Streak day: ___ / 60
  - Tomorrow's target: ___

### 21:00 — SHUT DOWN
No work after 21:00. No exceptions. Sleep is recovery. Tomorrow is another war.
