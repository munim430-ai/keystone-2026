## University Selector (masters, IELTS 5.5 entry across the board)
| If the student… | Route to | Because |
|---|---|---|
| Graduated >5 years ago | NOT Gachon (Dongshin/aSSIST/Kyungsung) | Gachon requires graduation since 2020–21 |
| Budget-first, bank can only reach ~$16k | Dongshin | Only $16k–20k bank floor in the pack |
| IELTS 6.5+ | Dongshin (50% off) or Sejong UG (50%) | Steepest IELTS ladders |
| Wants multi-semester certainty | aSSIST (50% × 3 sem) or Kyungsung MA (40% guaranteed @ GPA 3.0+) | Others are 1st-semester-only |
| Bachelor with SSC/HSC < 3.50 | NOT Kyungsung bachelor | Hard academic floor |
| Bachelor with IELTS < 6.5 | NOT Sejong UG (Kyungsung takes 5.5) | Sejong UG floor is 6.5 |
| Year-3 transfer | Expect NO Sejong scholarship | Explicitly excluded |

## Bank & Financial Thresholds
- Standard: **USD 20,000, 6-month statement** (Kyungsung, Sejong, Gachon, aSSIST-strict)
- Dongshin: **$16k–20k** | Gachon adds: **annual income ≥ 20,00,000 BDT** + parents' income certificate
- Bank statement/solvency/SOP: **never notarize**. Identity/tax/employment docs: **translate → notarize**.
- Sponsor name spelling must match bank records exactly — Gachon rejects on spelling mismatch.

## Attestation Router
- Academic certs for DHL set → **online MyGovBD attestation**
- Academic certs for Sejong → **e-apostille** (different chain!)
- NOC + financial affidavit → **stamp paper + Notary Public** (৳100 + ৳50 pages observed)

## Tells & Smells (rejection-risk signals)
- Gap year with no job certificate → Gachon immigration check fails
- SOP and cover letter give different "why Korea" → interview probing
- One-parent NOC → conspicuous; use both parents
- Missing return-to-Bangladesh clause anywhere → overstay suspicion
- Part-time-job enthusiasm in interview → contradicts sponsor affidavit

## Interview Protocol (10 seconds before it starts)
Display name = SURNAME GivenName (+App ID) · formal shirt/tie · quiet clean background · stable connection · passport + IELTS + academics physically at hand · mock interview attended (mandatory)

## Embassy Stage Quick Facts
- Cover letter → Visa Officer, Embassy of Korea, 4 Madani Ave, Dhaka-1212; 5 sections; sponsor named in conclusion
- TB test: approved clinic (sample: Prova Health Care, Banani); 8 AM–6 PM; report in 24 hrs; bring passport + 2 photocopies + 2 photos
- Photos: Korean size 35×45 mm — stock 5 copies minimum
