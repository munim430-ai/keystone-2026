# Chapter 5: Gachon, Dongshin & aSSIST — Master's (E-VISA) Fact Sheets

## Core Idea
Three graduate e-visa options spanning three price/positioning tiers: Gachon (Seongnam, strict eligibility), Dongshin (Naju, cheapest with IELTS-laddered scholarships), and aSSIST (Seoul, fee-waived boutique with a flat 50% scholarship).

## Gachon University — Seongnam | Graduate (E-VISA)
- **Application fee:** 120,000 KRW | **Major:** Business Administration (English track) — 4,993,000 KRW/semester
- **Eligibility (all must hold):** IELTS 5.5+ · CGPA 3.00+ · **graduated within the last 5 years (since 2020–2021)** · clear, strong motivation for the major · demonstrable financial capability
- **Scholarship:** 50% tuition reduction for the first semester
- **Documents:** application form · SSC/HSC/undergraduate certificate & transcript · passport copies · photo · applicant's + parents' ID · FRC · English certificate (TOEFL PBT 530/CBT 197/iBT 71, IELTS 5.5, CEFR B2) · study plan · **study-gap validation (job experience certificate — immigration WILL check)**
- **Financial capability (must demonstrate):** bank statement **USD 20,000+** · parents' income certificate (Trade, TIN & TAX, acknowledgement or employment certificate) · **annual income 20,00,000 BDT** · **financial documents must match sponsor's name exactly — a spelling mistake is not acceptable**

## Dongshin University — Naju | Master's (E-VISA)
- **Application fee:** 100,000 KRW | **Language:** IELTS 5.5+, TOEFL iBT 80+
- **Tuition/semester:** Software Convergence (Natural Science) 3,999,000 KRW; Global Business / TESOL (Social Science) 3,717,000 KRW
- **Scholarship (1st semester), IELTS-laddered:** 8.0 = 70% | 7.0 = 60% | 6.5 = 50% | 6.0 = 40% | 5.5 = 30%
- **Documents:** passport · Alien Card (holders) · CV · certificates/transcripts (Bachelor/HSC/SSC) · IELTS · parents' NID · FRC · **bank min $16k–$20k, 6 months** · SOP · photo · TB test report · email address

## aSSIST University — Seoul | Master's (E-VISA)
- **Application fee: WAIVED** | **Majors:** Global Tech, Innovation & Entrepreneurship — ~5,000,000 KRW (September intake)
- **Scholarship:** IELTS 5.5 & above = flat **50% for three semesters** (not just one — unique in this pack)
- **Documents:** same as Dongshin but **bank $20,000+ (6 months), STRICT**

## Decision Rules
- Graduate >5 years ago → Gachon is out; route to Dongshin/aSSIST/Kyungsung.
- Study gap of any kind → prepare the job-experience certificate BEFORE applying to Gachon; immigration verifies it.
- Budget-first family → Dongshin (lowest tuition, $16k bank floor); IELTS 6.5+ makes it dramatically cheaper (50%+ off).
- Multi-semester cost sensitivity → aSSIST's 3-semester 50% beats one-semester scholarships elsewhere despite higher sticker price.

## Key Takeaways
1. Every university in this trio takes IELTS 5.5 — the differentiators are eligibility rules, bank floor, and scholarship shape.
2. Gachon has the strictest paperwork discipline in the whole pack: name-spelling matching and study-gap verification are explicit rejection grounds.
3. Scholarship duration matters as much as percentage — always compute the multi-semester total for the family.
