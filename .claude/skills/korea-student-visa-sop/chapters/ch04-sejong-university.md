# Chapter 4: Sejong University (Seoul) — Undergraduate & Graduate Fact Sheet

## Core Idea
Sejong is the Seoul-brand option with a higher bar: IELTS 6.5 for undergraduate, e-apostilled academics, higher fees — and an unusual requirement to hand over an email address with password.

## Undergraduate
- **Application fee:** 150,000 KRW
- **Language:** IELTS 6.5+ | TOEFL iBT 71+ | New TEPS 327+
- **Normal-visa majors (all 4,556,000 KRW/semester):** Global Leadership (International Affairs), Public Administration, Media and Communication, Business Administration, Economics, Hospitality and Tourism Management; Music (Professional Music) 6,237,000 KRW
- **Regional-visa majors (6,186,000 KRW):** College of Convergence (IT) — Computer Science and Engineering, Creative Studies
- **Scholarship (1st semester, Year-1 entrants only — NOT Year-3 transfers):** IELTS 5.5+ = 30% | 6.5+ = 50% | 8.0+ = 80%

## Graduate
- **Application fee:** 170,000 KRW
- **Language:** IELTS 5.5+ | TOEFL iBT 71+ | New TEPS 327+
- **Tuition/semester:** Public Administration (Public Policy) 7,159,000 KRW; Hospitality and Tourism Management 7,660,000 KRW
- **Scholarship (1st semester):** IELTS 5.5+ = 30%

## Required Documents (both levels)
Passport · Alien Registration Card (holders only) · photo · certificate & transcript of Bachelor/HSC/SSC — **E-APOSTILLED required** · IELTS · parents' NID copies · applicant's NID and birth certificate · FRC · SOP · **email address WITH password (must)** · **bank statement & solvency: min 20,000 USD, 6-month statement** · (graduate adds CV)

## Decision Rules
- Undergraduate applicant below IELTS 6.5 → Sejong undergrad is out (contrast: Kyungsung takes 5.5).
- Transfer (Year-3) applicants get NO scholarship at Sejong — the cost pitch only works for Year-1 entry.
- The IELTS 8.0 = 80% scholarship makes Sejong the best deal in the pack for a top scorer — cheaper than "cheaper" universities after discount.

## Anti-patterns
- **Quoting the scholarship to a transfer student** — explicitly excluded; sets up a broken promise.
- **Sending MyGovBD-attested (non-apostilled) academics** — Sejong wants e-apostille; wrong chain = document rejection.

## Key Takeaways
1. Sejong's brand premium is real but so is its bar: 6.5 IELTS undergrad, apostille chain, biggest application fees in the pack.
2. Graduate tuition (7.1–7.6M KRW) is ~40–70% above Kyungsung's — the family must see the total-cost comparison.
3. The email+password requirement is unusual; set up a dedicated application email per student rather than surrendering a personal one.
