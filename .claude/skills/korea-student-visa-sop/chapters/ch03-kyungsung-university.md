# Chapter 3: Kyungsung University (Busan) — Bachelor, Master's & PhD Fact Sheet

## Core Idea
Kyungsung (Busan) is the workhorse option: IELTS 5.5 entry, generous IELTS-tiered scholarships, USD 20,000 bank requirement, and both e-visa (graduate) and regional-visa (engineering) tracks.

## Bachelor / Bachelor Regional
- **Application fee:** 80,000 KRW | **Dorm:** ~900,000 KRW per 4 months
- **Language:** IELTS 5.5+ | TOEFL 530 (CBT 197, iBT 71) | CEFR B2 | TEPS 600+
- **Academic:** SSC 3.50+ AND HSC 3.50+
- **Tuition/semester:** Humanities & Social Science (Global Business Administration, Global Hospitality Management, Global Korean Studies) — 3,296,000 KRW; School of Global Engineering (Global Mechanical Design Engineering, Global IT Engineering) — 4,321,000 KRW
- **Regional visa ONLY available for:** Global Mechanical Design Engineering, Global Business Administration, Global Hospitality Management, Global IT Engineering
- **Scholarship (1st semester only):** IELTS 5.5–6.5 = 30% | 7.0 = 40% | 7.5 = 50%

## Master's (E-VISA) & PhD (E-VISA)
- **Application fee:** 80,000 KRW | Language same as bachelor
- **Master's tuition/semester:** Global Business Administration 4,454,000 KRW; Global Hospitality Management 4,454,000 KRW; Global Engineering (Mechanical Design / IT) 5,545,000 KRW
- **PhD tuition/semester:** Global Hospitality Management / Global Business — 4,608,000 KRW
- **Scholarship:** 1st semester IELTS 5.5+ = 45% (KSU alumni 55%); semesters 2–4 = 40–100% by academic performance, **40% guaranteed with GPA 3.00+**

## Required Documents (bachelor)
Passport · HSC & SSC certificate/transcript · IELTS · SOP · applicant's + parents' NID · FRC & birth certificate · financial documents (TIN, TAX, trade/job certificate) · **bank: minimum 6-month statement with 20,000 USD** · email & phone · photo · chosen major
(Master's adds: CV, bachelor certificate/transcript, Alien Card if holder; drops the TIN/TAX bundle — see Ch 2 note that TIN/TAX/Trade are not mandatory for Kyungsung.)

## Decision Rules
- Bachelor applicant with SSC/HSC below 3.50 → Kyungsung bachelor is out; look elsewhere.
- IELTS 7.0+ applicant → Kyungsung bachelor scholarship jumps to 40–50%; use this in cost negotiations with the family.
- Master's with GPA 3.00+ → pitch the guaranteed 40% continuing scholarship — it changes the 2-year cost math.

## Key Takeaways
1. Kyungsung = lowest-friction full-stack option (bachelor→PhD) with an interview question bank already tuned to it (Ch 9).
2. Engineering majors carry both higher tuition AND the regional-visa restriction — flag to students expecting Seoul.
3. The 20,000 USD / 6-month bank rule is non-negotiable and identical across levels.
