# Glossary

**Alien Registration Card (ARC)** — Korean residence ID; required in applications only from applicants already holding one (Ch 3, Ch 4, Ch 5)
**Apostille / E-Apostille** — international document authentication; Sejong requires e-apostilled academic certificates, distinct from MyGovBD attestation (Ch 2, Ch 4)
**Application fee** — non-refundable fee paid at submission; observed range 80,000–170,000 KRW; waived at aSSIST (Ch 1, Ch 3–5)
**Attestation (MyGovBD)** — online government attestation of academic certificates for the DHL set (Ch 2)
**Bank statement floor** — minimum balance held 6 months: USD 20,000 standard; Dongshin accepts $16k–20k; Gachon/aSSIST/Sejong/Kyungsung hold at $20k (Ch 3–5)
**CoA (Certificate of Admission)** — official admission certificate issued after document verification and tuition payment; the visa trigger (Ch 1)
**Cover letter** — one-page, 5-section letter to the Visa Officer fronting the embassy file (Ch 7)
**D-2 visa** — the Korean student visa category for degree study; the subject of the cover letter (Ch 7)
**Display-name rule** — video-interview name must be passport order, surname first, plus Application ID if required (Ch 9)
**DHL set** — the physical, notarized document bundle couriered to the university, distinct from the online set (Ch 2)
**E-VISA track** — electronic visa processing used by Kyungsung (masters/PhD), Gachon, Dongshin, aSSIST (Ch 3, Ch 5)
**Financial affidavit** — 3-page notarized "Sponsorship and Financial Declaration and Property Valuation": sponsorship + property tables + income tables in one (Ch 8)
**FRC (Family Relation Certificate)** — proves the parent-child link; required by every university sheet (Ch 2–5)
**Khatian / Dag / Mouza / J.L. No.** — Bangladeshi land-record identifiers required in the affidavit's immovable-property table (Ch 8)
**Korean-size photo** — 35×45 mm photo; keep 5 copies (Ch 2)
**Mock (trial) interview** — mandatory rehearsal before the real interview (Ch 9)
**NOC (Affidavit of No Objection)** — both parents' notarized declaration consenting to study in Korea and affirming return (Ch 8)
**Regional visa** — visa category restricted to designated majors (Kyungsung engineering; Sejong IT college) (Ch 3, Ch 4)
**Return clause** — the "will return to Bangladesh after completion" undertaking; closes both affidavits and the SOP (Ch 6, Ch 8)
**Solvency certificate** — bank-issued solvency proof accompanying the statement; never notarized (Ch 2)
**SOP (Statement of Purpose)** — the 9-paragraph personal narrative; not notarized; must be consistent with all other documents (Ch 6)
**Sponsor** — the person (typically the father) who declares financial responsibility; name must match across affidavit, bank documents, cover letter, and interview answers (Ch 5, Ch 7–9)
**Study-gap validation** — job-experience certificate justifying any gap years; Gachon states immigration will verify (Ch 5, Ch 6)
**SWIFT copy** — proof of international tuition transfer sent to the university to trigger CoA (Ch 1)
**TB test** — tuberculosis test from an approved Dhaka clinic at embassy stage; 24-hour report; test hours 8 AM–6 PM (Ch 2)
**TIN / e-TIN** — tax identification; translated + notarized in the DHL set; not mandatory for Kyungsung (Ch 2, Ch 3)
