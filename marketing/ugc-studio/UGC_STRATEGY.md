## 1. Your business, from the repo

**Keystone Education** is a Korea-specialist study-abroad consultancy run by
founder Hasibul Munim (9 years lived in Korea) out of **Narsingdi Bazar +
Gazipur** — tier-2 Bangladesh, deliberately *not* Dhaka. It places Bangladeshi
students into South Korean universities (also Malaysia/Canada/UK), end-to-end,
for a **flat ৳120,000 with zero hidden fees, collected only after the visa** —
roughly half of what Dhaka incumbents (WeCare, Jenny, Roushan ~৳250–300k) charge,
and structurally cheaper because there's no Dhaka rent and document-checking is
automated.

**The unique value proposition** is not price — it's **verifiable honesty in a
fraud-soaked market.** The Financial Express (May 2026) documented an unregulated
industry where ~65% of applications are fraudulent; agencies quote a low base fee,
pile on hidden costs, take money upfront, and vanish. Keystone's entire moat is
being the one you can *check*: published price, "No Visa No Fee," IEQAS-verifiable
universities, a real local office you can walk into. The brand spine is literally
**"আমরা লুকাই না — আমরা শেখাই" (We don't hide — we teach).**

**The ICP is four buyers, one household** (master-plan Part 2):

| Buyer | Sees it on | Core fear | What converts them |
|-------|-----------|-----------|--------------------|
| **Student (18–24)** | Facebook/Reels | "Am I stuck without IELTS?" | adventure, no-IELTS route, part-time income |
| **Father (pays)** | price/ROI | "Is this a scam / a waste?" | total-cost transparency, ROI, fee-after-visa |
| **Mother (emotional)** | safety | "Will my child be safe & in touch?" | soft-landing, care, someone in Korea |
| **Uncle (financier)** | legitimacy | "Am I wiring money to a fraud?" | verifiable proof, IEQAS, fee-after-visa |

Primary segment: **tier-2 middle families** (Narsingdi/Gazipur/Mymensingh), high
price-sensitivity, *very* high trust-need. Content must hit all four buyers because
they decide together.

## 2. One honest warning before the angles (read this)

You referenced the "AI club server **fake testimonial**" format. For almost any
other business I'd say go for it. **For this business, a fabricated testimonial —
an AI-generated "student" saying "Keystone got me my visa!" presented as real — is
the single most dangerous format you could post**, for three reasons:

1. **It is exactly what your competitors do.** Your whole growth thesis is that the
   market is drowning in fake proof and you are the verifiable alternative. Faking a
   testimonial makes you a member of the cartel you're attacking.
2. **Tier-2 is a small world.** In Narsingdi people know each other. A "student" no
   one can find is noticed, and the story becomes "Keystone fakes students."
3. **It's deceptive advertising** and it contradicts your own published rules
   ("never post PII/claims you can't verify", "no 98% success"). The guardrails in
   this studio **block it in code.**

The good news: you don't need it. The formats below are just as scroll-stopping and
they *compound* your trust moat instead of spending it. When you want a real
testimonial, film a **consented real student** (studio supports it via a consent
flag) — that's worth ten fakes.

## 3. The 5 niche UGC angles (format + hook)

Each is niche to *your* ICP, not generic "study abroad" content. All 20 ready-to-
render scripts in `scripts.yaml` are instances of these.

### Angle 1 — "IELTS ছাড়াই কোরিয়া" (No-IELTS route) · **STUDENT**
- **Format:** faceless text-motion Reel (CPU, today). Big Bangla kinetic captions.
- **Hook:** *"IELTS নাই? কোরিয়া তবুও সম্ভব।"* ("No IELTS? Korea is still possible.")
- **Why it wins:** highest-intent hook in the whole market — it's the exact fear
  that stops tier-2 students, and almost no competitor leads with the EAP answer.

### Angle 2 — "৫টি প্রশ্ন / যাচাই করুন" (Whistleblower checklist) · **FATHER + UNCLE**
- **Format:** carousel-as-Reel; 5 quick caption cards; cite Financial Express, name
  no competitor.
- **Hook:** *"টাকা দেওয়ার আগে এই ৫টা প্রশ্ন করুন।"* ("Ask these 5 questions before
  paying a taka.")
- **Why it wins:** educational content gets *shared* (every share is a free ad), and
  it frames you as the only honest player. Straight from your whistleblower pillars.

### Angle 3 — "আমাদের বিশ্বাস করবেন না — যাচাই করুন" (Verifiable-proof flex) · **UNCLE**
- **Format:** screen-recording of `studyinkorea.go.kr` + an admit letter (real,
  consented) / IEQAS entry. The anti-testimonial.
- **Hook:** *"আমাদের কথা বিশ্বাস করবেন না।"* ("Don't believe our words.")
- **Why it wins:** in a market of fake proof, telling the financier to verify *you*
  is disarming and unique. This is the format that replaces the fake testimonial.

### Angle 4 — "সস্তা কেন — প্রতারণা নয়" (Why-cheaper reframe) · **FATHER**
- **Format:** faceless explainer, 3 reasons.
- **Hook:** *"এত কম কেন? সন্দেহ হচ্ছে?"* ("Why so cheap? Suspicious?")
- **Why it wins:** neutralizes the #1 objection your own plan flags — that cheap
  reads as scam. Reframes cheap as *efficient*, not desperate.

### Angle 5 — "মায়ের চিন্তা / আপনার নারসিংদিতেই" (Safety + local) · **MOTHER**
- **Format:** warm, softer pace; local office / Marina (consented) as the local face.
- **Hook:** *"সন্তান বিদেশে — নিরাপদ থাকবে তো?"* ("Child abroad — will they be safe?")
- **Why it wins:** the mother is the emotional decision-maker no competitor speaks
  to, and "right here in Narsingdi, not a Dhaka stranger" is a first-mover local moat.

**Bonus B2B angle** (for the 859-center IELTS list + colleges): partnership /
free-seminar hooks — see `b01`, `b02` in `scripts.yaml`. These target center owners,
not students, and dovetail with the voice-agent that calls the same list.

## 4. Format → tool → cost map

Start with the four $0 CPU formats. They are also the highest-trust ones — which,
for this brand, are the highest-converting ones. Add voice/avatar/b-roll later.

## 5. Publishing cadence (fits the existing calendar)

Slot these into `marketing/content/posting-calendar.md` and schedule via **Postiz**:
1 Reel/day, rotating personas Mon→student, Tue→father, Wed→uncle(proof),
Thu→mother, Fri→B2B/seminar. The studio writes each video's caption to
`out/<id>.caption.txt` for one-click scheduling. **Human-QA every Bangla caption
before publish** (base Whisper Bangla WER is high) — same guardrail as the rest of
the marketing stack.
